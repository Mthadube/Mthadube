About Ultimate TS Financial Services


We are an online financial services company. We provide affordable loans against your salary. We offer a minimum loan amount from R500 to R5000. Once off service Fee is R70. Initiation fee is R150.

With higher acceptance than traditional lenders and repayments taken directly from your paycheck, Salary Finance makes borrowing the money you need easier than ever.





Payment Terms


  •     Collection all payments are done via debit order.
  •   One month circle for repayment plus 30% interest.

Application Criteria for a salary loan

  •   You must be 18 and older, have a valid South African Bank Account, have a valid South African ID and should be permanently employed
  •   You will be required to provide proof of income; 3 months recent payslips & 3 months recent bank account statements
  •   Even if you took and paid up a loan same creteria will be requested on borrowing
